# Design System

## Product

Camp Gan Israel Sports Complex is a campaign site for a near-complete sports campus. The site should feel premium, generous, and direct: cinematic sports-campus imagery, confident typography, restrained orange accents, and clear donation paths.

## Aesthetic Direction

Elegant campaign editorial. Use large real media, calm whitespace, strong but simple hierarchy, and very few decorative effects. The video and renders should carry the emotion; UI should support the campaign without looking like a generic template.

## Typography

- Display/Hero: Sora, heavy weights for campaign headlines and section titles.
- Body/UI: Plus Jakarta Sans for polished readability and cleaner navigation.
- Label text: Plus Jakarta Sans, uppercase sparingly, wide tracking only for small labels.

## Color

- Primary orange: `#ed7a1a`
- Highlight orange: `#f1953e`
- Deep navy: `#08111f`
- Text navy: `#0f172a`
- Warm page background: `#f6f7f3`
- White: `#ffffff`

Use orange only for active state, donation calls to action, campaign totals, and key fundraising terms. Avoid blue/purple gradients and generic decorative blobs.

## Layout

- Desktop nav is centered. The Donate Now CTA sits independently on the right so the menu does not drift.
- Home stays cinematic: full-screen video, minimal hero copy, large campaign lines, and one concise progress panel.
- Interior page heroes should be shorter than the homepage and should not overpower the page content.
- Content sections should be open editorial layouts or single-purpose cards, not cards inside cards.
- Repeated cards use `rounded-md` and subtle shadows only.

## Motion

Use short fade/slide motion on entrance only. Avoid distracting hover movement on large content sections.

## Quality Bar

Every page should pass a two-second scan: what page am I on, what matters, and what should I click next. If a section feels like filler, reduce visual noise rather than adding decoration.
